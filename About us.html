Our Mission

Building a community through sports.

Ensuring quality and accessibility for all.

Fostering sportsmanship and teamwork.

Providing secure and well-maintained grounds.

Purpose and Community Building: The mission of the Sherubtse College Ground Booking platform is to bring the college community together through sports. We aim to
create an accessible space for students, faculty, and the surrounding community to engage in physical activity, fostering a spirit of teamwork, sportsmanship, and inclusivity.

Commitment to Quality: Our platform is dedicated to maintaining high standards in our grounds and facilities, ensuring that each space is secure, well-kept, and
readily available for those eager to participate in various sports activities.

Inclusion and Accessibility: At Sherubtse College, we believe everyone should have access to quality sports facilities. Our mission is to provide a seamless booking
experience that encourages active involvement from all members of our community.

Our Facilities

Diverse Sports Offerings: Our grounds are designed to cater to a range of sports including football, basketball, volleyball, and more. Each area is equipped to meet the
standards of these sports, whether it’s the smooth, green turf of the football field or the well-marked courts for volleyball and basketball.

Accessibility and Convenience: Our facilities are easily accessible and designed for players of all skill levels. The booking process is streamlined for users, allowing
students and teams to reserve spaces for practice, tournaments, and recreational play with ease.

Continuous Improvement: We listen to feedback and are committed to upgrading and maintaining our facilities to keep up with the needs of our active college community.
